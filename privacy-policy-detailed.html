MCR Partners (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the mobile game Ultimate Fighting Simulator. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in through Facebook Login, Google Sign-In, Apple Sign‑In, or via Firebase email login, we may collect:

  • Your name (if provided by the login provider)
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (if permission is granted)
  • Authentication User ID (UID)

Firebase Authentication may also send operational emails such as: verification emails, password reset emails, security notifications, and account recovery emails.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect data through the App and integrated services:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language settings)
  • IP address and general location (approximate, not precise)
  • In‑app activity, gameplay metrics, progression, events, and session duration
  • Error logs, crash reports, and performance data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID) used by AdMob

1.3 Payment & Purchase Information

All payments are processed by:

  • Apple App Store (iOS users)
  • Google Play Store (Android users)

We do not store or handle credit card numbers. Purchase receipts and entitlements are verified and managed through RevenueCat, which securely communicates with the app stores to confirm purchases.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Sync gameplay progress across devices
  • Process and validate in‑app purchases
  • Serve ads via AdMob (personalized or non‑personalized depending on your region/consent)
  • Improve game performance and user experience through analytics
  • Detect fraud, prevent cheating, and ensure player security
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with applicable laws and app store requirements

3. Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID)
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Ad interactions (views, clicks, impressions)
  • Diagnostic and performance information

In regions such as the EU, the App may display a consent form through Google’s UMP (User Messaging Platform), allowing users to choose personalized or non‑personalized ads.

Google’s ad policies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users engage with the App. Firebase may collect:

  • General usage trends

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Third‑Party Services Used
